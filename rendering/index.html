Renderings




An ongoing series of renderings that are often results of my industrial design process. Modeled in Solidworks, rendered in Keyshot unless noted.

With each rendering, I aim to explore different aspects of materiality & atmosphere, and its affect on whatever my design might be.

Bench frame, 2018

I once heard someone describe designing public furniture as saying it has to be nice enough to be in public, but not nice enough that someone won't sit on it. I thought that was a pretty interesting yet conflicting set of criteria. I think people may find some kind of comfort in things that feel a bit arbitrary?
