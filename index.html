  • About Me

    • t 21 i



      t 21 i is an excersise in working with a semi-fictional context in mind and looking elsewhere for furniture forms- in hopes of bringing you elsewhere.

      Polypropylene, ABS, steel and nylon.

    • Garden Chair



      A garden chair that's meant to co-exist amongst the lawn / yard landscape. Legs and back are constructed as solid bent laminations.

      Dyed ash.

    • timzarras.me



      I set up timzarras.me to act as an online sketchbook for web projects and other things.

    • Container



      1 week assignment to utilize vacuum forming. Aimed to be something formally characteristic to manufactured plastic containers.

      Polystyrene and found cap.

    • Connection Study



      This project is about being iterative with a discovered connection, as well as an inherent study in gesture and stance from the resulting forms / structures.

      Tubular steel and cold rolled steel rod.

    • Fan



      Fan made for our "container" assignment in Sophomore Studio. A container for dismembered fan components.

      Hard maple and fan components.
