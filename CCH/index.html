Cubic Cable Holder is designed to accomplish a singular task: anchoring charging cables that will otherwise fall off the table when unplugged. The product is precisely machined from solid metals, followed by a proprietary manual lapping polish process to give the surfaces a lustrous finish.

We are convinced by things that show internal complexity, that show the traces of an interesting evolution. Daily use of the product imbues it a mysteriously elegant patina over time. It provides utility while involves you as part of its completion.
